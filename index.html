How many decimal places do you want.

1st and 2nd are any numbers form 0 to 100000 with which you can perform a mathematical operation

+: is for addition like 1 + 1 = 2

-: is for subtraction like 1 - 1 = 0

/: is for division like 1 / 1 = 1

*: is for multiplication like 1 * 1 = 1

**: is for exponent like 2 **3 = 2 * 2 * 2 therefore 8

%: is for remainder like 10 % 3 = 1

Reset: is to clear recent operations
